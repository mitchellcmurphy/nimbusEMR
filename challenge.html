THE CHALLENGE

Technologists. Innovators. Dreamers. The Shape Challenge is an open contest to inspire, showcase, and celebrate pure technological innovation.

Have a groundbreaking technology that can augment a network? Creating tech that can make our cities smarter? Adding to the Internet of Things with an entirely new ”Thing”?

Submit your innovation today, and you’ll have the chance to win $50,000, gain buzz for your project, and pitch live on stage. For dates check the schedule.

CATEGORIES

Augmented Networks

AT&T has a vision for a network that goes beyond connecting two points—a network with ever-growing intelligence and capabilities. And we need your help to build it.

How to win: Wow us with a solution to a network or content delivery challenge, whether it’s mobile, satellite, Wi-Fi, or wireline. We’ll consider all submissions that use or contribute to AT&T technologies.

Topic examples: Software Defined Network (SDN): We’re excited to see technologies that can help us rethink how we manage network services. Micro-services: We’re looking for micro-services to enable connections across multiple containers to activate virtual network functions on an as-needed basis. Think about optimizing content delivery, improving M2M communications, or building an application distribution system.

Virtual Reality (VR) Delivery: We want to support and develop technologies that help with the creation, optimization, and delivery of VR content while also encouraging widespread VR adoption.

Connected Things

Smartphones have been game-changers, but they represent just a fraction of the things that will keep the world around us connected. New devices and entirely new ways of experiencing tech prove we’ve only just begun. The next generation of connected things will deliver high-convenience, boundless capabilities, and an awe-inspiring vision of the future.

How to win: Excite us with connected tech that explores new forms and unexpected functions. This new “thing” can connect through mobile, satellite, Wi-Fi, or wireline. We’ll consider submissions that use or contribute to AT&T technologies such as M2X or AT&T-certified modules.

Topic examples: Smart Textiles: Connected Clothing introduces the latest technologies into everyday materials like bandages, clothing, shoes, or sportswear. We want to see innovative techniques that seamlessly weave tech into our gear.

Wearables: Let’s uncover the next groundbreaking step in wearable products—big or small.

Industrial Robotics: Show us how your robotic solution augments human capabilities, or offloads mundane or dangerous tasks and activities.

Drones: Connected drones are making people’s lives easier, more convenient, and safer. Let’s find new ways to utilize this technology.

Smart Cities

The cities of tomorrow are at the heart of a more sustainable future filled with continued economic growth and new job creation—and city leaders have taken note. Now is the time to explore how technology can be integrated for cost reductions, citizen well-being, sustainability and economic development.

How to win: Inspire us with technology that solves for one of the countless challenges cities face. We’ll consider submissions that use or contribute to AT&T technologies like M2X and Flow Designer.

Topic examples: Energy & Utilities Management: From water management to smart grid and smart lighting technology, we’re looking for tech-based solutions that ensure a city’s critical resources are managed effectively.

Transportation: Route optimization, smart parking and metering, and connected vehicle technology are just some of the ways we can impact a city’s transportation system.

Infrastructure: From smart building solutions to environmental monitoring, the infrastructure of a city creates the framework for development and growth. We want to explore technologies that integrate the physical, digital, and communicative infrastructures that form a city.
