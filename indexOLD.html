What is the 2016 AT&T Developer Summit?

The AT&T Developer Summit is a developer-focused event, packed with valuable news and information about emerging technologies, products and services and the best place to network with top industry professionals and experts.

Event Highlights

  • Track Sessions

    Wearables, Internet of Things, WebRTC and App Strategies are just a few of the track session topics you can join. Hand-pick your sessions to plan your day and get the most out of the Summit.

  • Expo Hall

    Spend time in our exhibition hall to interact with sponsors and AT&T experts. Get hands-on experience, demonstrations and in-depth information on the latest technology, devices, and more.

  • Rock @ Rain

    Rock @ Rain is our exclusive concert for Summit attendees with past performances from big names like The Killers, Neon Trees, and Macklemore; you’ll never guess who we’ve got lined up to take the stage this year.

  • AT&T Executive Panels

    AT&T executives will host sessions and panels to discuss the latest trends, offer their predictions and answer your questions about the Internet of Things (IoT), connected car and home and other emerging technologies.

  • Mentorship Program

    Our mentorships were so popular last year that we’ve expanded them! This year you can listen to industry insights at a Futurecast or sign up for a private session to get some one-on-one attention.

  • Celebrity Speaker

    We’ve flown in some special talent to speak on state of tech and its impact on you. We’re keeping the identity of our speaker under wraps for now, but we’ll be announcing it before the big day so check back often.
