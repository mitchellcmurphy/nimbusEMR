1st Round

All apps will undergo an initial screening where judging of your solution will be based on the criteria below and weighted accordingly.

2nd Round

The top 20 teams will win the right to pitch their apps on stage on Sunday and be evaluated by our panel of expert guest judges.

Final Round

The top two teams from Las Vegas and the top team from Mexico will pitch their solutions on stage at the AT&T Summit Keynote, where the audience will be able to vote for their favorite solutions!
