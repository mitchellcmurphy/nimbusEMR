Maker Camp Live

OVERVIEW

Did you think Shape was just for adults? We are excited to have a project to entertain and engage the whole family.

Maker Camp LIVE is designed for kids who are inspired by hacking, tinkering, learning, crafting, and making. It combines the best parts of summer camp with starter projects. In a fun and family-friendly environment, kids learn to make things and use them in future or current projects.

PROJECTS

LEARNING TO SOLDER
July 16
10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Required for all ages

Have you ever wanted to see what's at the bottom of your bag, but just don't have enough light? This super neat project adds a strip of RGB LEDs to the inside of a canvas tote bag, which will automatically light up when you open the bag!

SAFETY WAIVER

DOWNLOAD AND PRINT SAFETY WAIVER

Safety waiver must be signed at the time of on-site registration. If the parent is not present and children are with a guardian, you must have the parent print and sign the safety waiver attached. If there is no valid safety waiver signed by the parent the child may not participate in these projects for safety reasons.
