Exhibits

Overview

People can explore various areas to interact with cutting-edge demos, listen to a visionary’s thoughts on the future, or participate in a town hall discussion or workshop. Developers curious about the next big thing, content creators interested in creating VR video, neighborhood advocates wondering how technology can improve their city, and people who love playing with the latest technology, will find plenty to explore.

FOCUS AREAS

Internet of Things

Subtitle

We have seen the profound impact that mobile networks and connectivity have had, but there is still much more to come. Software defined networking, content delivery networks, and microservices are revolutionizing network operations and will unlock a range of new capabilities. Attendees can also learn about other hot network topics such as next generation WAN/LAN, privacy/security, Big Data, and the cloud.

Innovation of Wearables

Subtitle

People are becoming accustomed to smartwatches and fitness devices, but there is a wealth of fascinating technology that will impact people in deeper ways.  Sensor functionality is rapidly increasing and leading to some incredible new devices and clothing. Developers can learn how to participate in the segment while general attendees and kids can test out the latest wearables.

Smart Cities

Subtitle

Technology can improve communities in many ways, from transportation to energy management to public safety. For these solutions to best meet the needs of the community, it is important for citizens to be involved and provide input. Smart Cities are also creating complex issues that developers need to solve.
