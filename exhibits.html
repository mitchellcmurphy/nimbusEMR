Internet of Things
Subtitle

We have seen the profound impact that mobile networks and connectivity have had, but there is still much more to come. Innovation of Wearables
Subtitle

People are becoming accustomed to smartwatches and fitness devices, but there is a wealth of fascinating technology that will impact people in deeper ways. Sensor functionality is rapidly increasing and leading to some incredible new devices and clothing. Developers can learn how to participate in the segment while general attendees and kids can test out the latest wearables.

Next Generation Video
Subtitle

Video is on track to make up 80% of Internet traffic. Combined with consumers increasing desire to consume content everywhere, the next step in the evolution of the medium is just around the corner. Virtual reality and augmented reality are on the verge of massive growth, but there are challenges for developers and independent content creators. In this area, video innovators can learn about VR/AR best practices.
